About this area

The heart of Miami's financial district.

Right on Brickell Avenue, Pura Vida moves in step with one of the city's most dynamic neighborhoods. Office towers fill early, banking headquarters hum to life, and professionals flow between meetings, lunch breaks, and the next item on the calendar — a sharp, walkable stretch that defines urban Miami.

We're a few minutes' walk from Mary Brickell Village and Brickell City Centre, one block from the Brickell Metromover, and a regular morning stop for the Millecento, 500 Brickell, and Icon Brickell crowds.
